Just the right food
The main feature of these cafes is nothing fried. As a heat treatment - only baking. Everything is prepared by hand according to our own recipes and taking into account the principles of separate nutrition. The dishes are free of mayonnaise, vinegar, preservatives and additives. Diet food, reduced in fat, salt and sugar. There are vegetarian options. Any food can be taken away. Some business centers have Healthy Food vending machines with cheese cakes, salads and rolls.
Fine ingredients
Falafel, hummus, pilaf, porridge, soups - everything is made from fruits and vegetables. The atmosphere is welcoming and friendly: if you find the necessary products in the fridge, the chef will be able to prepare a signature dish for you to order. The cafe is small, located on the territory of the Artplay space and therefore there may not be a free table during rush hours.
